Formalise all results in this paper as Lean 4 theorems and prove them. The paper is provided as note.tex in this project.

State each result faithfully and type-correctly — do not weaken, strengthen, or otherwise alter a statement to make its proof easier. If a complete proof is out of reach, leave a `sorry` on the faithful statement rather than proving a different theorem.

If, while formalising, you find an actual error in one of the paper's proofs (a step that does not follow, a false lemma, a missing hypothesis), try to fix it. Record what was wrong, and how you fixed it or why you couldn't, in a file named `ERROR.md` at the top level of the project. Only write `ERROR.md` for genuine mathematical errors in the paper — not for ordinary formalization difficulty.
